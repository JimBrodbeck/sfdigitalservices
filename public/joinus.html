We’re Hiring Technologists

As the Innovation Capital of the world, San Francisco is a vibrant beautiful city that is one of the world’s foremost cultural, commercial, and financial centers. San Francisco provides a broad range of services to residents and visitors.

San Francisco does a lot of things well, but like most places, we know that San Francisco has its own challenges. We know that we can do a better job of listening to you, our customers. To do this better, we are creating a Digital Services Team to transform how the City provides services to those that work, live, and play in San Francisco.

The Digital Services Team, led by the Chief Digital Services Officer (CDSO), will lead the City & County of San Francisco into the digital age. The team will partner with departments to modernize digital services citywide. Four fundamental principles guide this task: put the needs of residents first, focus on delivery and outcomes over process, promote an agile and data driven culture, and make City services accessible to everyone.

As a member of the team you'll help change culture and practices, as well as roll your sleeves up for hands-on work on the development, operation, and ongoing improvement of complex digital services.

The Content Strategist and User Experience Designer will conduct a thorough, user-centered assessment of the Mayor's Office of Housing and Community Development's web content at sfmohcd.org.
