Come Build a Digital City

We’re Hiring Technologists

As the Innovation Capital of the world, San Francisco is a vibrant beautiful city that is one of the world’s foremost cultural, commercial, and financial centers. San Francisco provides a broad range of services to residents and visitors.

San Francisco does a lot of things well, but like most places, we know that San Francisco has its own challenges. We know that we can do a better job of listening to you, our customers. To do this better, we are creating a central Digital Services Team, as well as Digital Services teams within major agencies, to transform how the City provides services to those that work, live, and play in San Francisco.

Technologists who join this effort will lead the City & County of San Francisco into the digital age, modernizing how we interact with residents citywide. Four fundamental principles guide this task: put the needs of residents first, focus on delivery and outcomes over process, promote an agile and data driven culture, and make City services accessible to everyone.

In these roles, you'll help change culture and practices, as well as roll your sleeves up for hands-on work on the development, operation, and ongoing improvement of complex digital services.

Design & Content

Senior Service Designer

San Francisco Digital Services Team

The Senior Service Designer will be a lead player in transforming the customer service experience of services across the City. In this role, you will be a champion for user needs, working with policy and program colleagues to redesign services from every angle. You will bring expert design, user research, and user testing skills to iteratively build better public services that are more simple, beautiful, and easy-to-use. We're looking for an experienced service designer to work collaboratively on a multi-disciplinary agile team.

Senior Content Strategist

San Francisco Digital Services Team

The Senior Content Strategist will lead a complete overhaul of the City's content practices. Not only will this person roll up their sleeves and write content, you will be responsible for creating a content strategy for the City and building a culture of excellent content design across all city services. We're looking for someone with a passion for inclusive, accessible language who has experience with user testing, content management best practices, and training and teaching others.

Service Designer or UX Researcher

Human Services Agency, Innovation Office

We're looking for another service designer or UX researcher to help over 200,000 San Franciscans living in poverty and abuse. Your expertise will focus on examining and improving how clients experience Agency services digitally, in person, by phone and mail. You will work with employees across the Agency to share service design methods while co-creating measurable improvements in internal and external processes to deliver services. Three years of service design or UX research experience required. Strong emotional intelligence, humility, and being a team player preferred. Candidates with diverse backgrounds and experiences welcome.

Visual Designer

Human Services Agency, Innovation Office

Are you a visual designer who wants to help the estimated one in four San Franciscans living in poverty and abuse? You will through walk internal and public processes for service delivery, visualize them as experience maps, and then collaborate with colleagues to implement measurable change. You will create infographics from dashboards and spreadsheets to help managers and analysts see new opportunities for improving services. Your expertise will also focus on improving forms, signage, and other materials the public experiences as part of their “touchpoints” with Agency services both online and in print. Three years of visual design experience required. Strong emotional intelligence, humility, and being a team player preferred. Candidates with diverse backgrounds and experiences welcome.

Content Strategist and UX Designer

Mayor's office of Housing and Community Development

The Content Strategist and User Experience Designer will conduct a thorough, user-centered assessment of critical content about affordable housing and City community development on the Mayor's Office site at sfmohcd.org. You will lead a site overhaul, redesigning the UX and information architecture so that navigation is simplified and makes sense, and making all content easy to read and understand. You'll also create and implement practices to ensure all content is current, correct, and non-duplicative; look & feel are consistent with City standards; and the content upkeep is sustainable by MOHCD staff. We're looking for someone with experience writing, user-testing, and iteratively improving content, and teaching others to do the same. Drupal experience a plus.

Data

Housing Data Specialist

Mayor's Office of Housing and Community Development

Our department has a large volume of critical housing placement and construction pipeline data, currently contained in Excel spreadsheets, Access data tables, and other formats. We need an expert in managing large, complex data sets to lead an evaluation, architect and implement an appropriate database design in Salesforce, and then manage the migration into Salesforce to make this important more accessible, and easy to use. You'll use your expertise to find insightful ways to make the data more useful for policymaking and improving service delivery of our affordable housing programs; collaborate with other ongoing Salesforce development efforts; and help create standards for effective user roles and permissions. Salesforce experience a must; no experience with affordable housing required.
