Come Build a Digital City

We’re Hiring Technologists

As the Innovation Capital of the world, San Francisco is a vibrant beautiful city that is one of the world’s foremost cultural, commercial, and financial centers. San Francisco provides a broad range of services to residents and visitors.

San Francisco does a lot of things well, but like most places, we know that San Francisco has its own challenges. We know that we can do a better job of listening to you, our customers. To do this better, we are creating a Digital Services Team to transform how the City provides services to those that work, live, and play in San Francisco.

The Digital Services Team, led by the Chief Digital Services Officer (CDSO), will lead the City & County of San Francisco into the digital age. The team will partner with departments to modernize digital services citywide. Four fundamental principles guide this task: put the needs of residents first, focus on delivery and outcomes over process, promote an agile and data driven culture, and make City services accessible to everyone.

As a member of the team you'll help change culture and practices, as well as roll your sleeves up for hands-on work on the development, operation, and ongoing improvement of complex digital services.

The Roles

Senior Service Designer

The Senior Service Designer will be a lead player in transforming the customer service experience through user-design. This person will use their skills with data, analysis, user research and project management to help build better public services that are more simple, beautiful, and easy-to-use.

Apply

Senior Content Strategist

The Senior Content Strategist will lead a complete overhaul of the City's content practices. Not only will this person roll up their sleeves and write content, they will be responsible for creating a content strategy for the City, and a culture of excellent content design across all city services. They will share your passion for inclusive, accessible language and set a high standard for the City to manage content in the long term.

Apply

Senior Software Engineer

We’re seeking a skilled full-stack web developer to join our team to work on City services that impact the lives of every San Franciscan, like affordable housing, small business permitting, job placement and workforce training programs, and more. You will serve as a technical authority on product teams, solving large, complex problems by putting the needs of users first.

Apply

Content Strategist and UX Designer

Housing and Community Development

The Content Strategist and User Experience Designer will conduct a thorough, user-centered assessment of the Mayor's Office of Housing and Community Development's web content at sfmohcd.org. They will lead an overhaul of the site to make all content easy to read and understand, as well as ensuring all content is current, correct, and non-duplicative. They will redesign the UX so that navigation is simplified and makes sense, look & feel are consistent with City standards, and the content upkeep is sustainable by MOHCD staff.
