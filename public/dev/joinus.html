As the Innovation Capital of the world, San Francisco is a vibrant beautiful city that is one of the world’s foremost cultural, commercial, and financial centers. San Francisco provides a broad range of services to residents and visitors.

San Francisco does a lot of things well, but like most places, we know that San Francisco has its own challenges. We know that we can do a better job of listening to you, our customers. To do this better, we are creating a central Digital Services Team, as well as Digital Services teams within major agencies, to transform how the City provides services to those that work, live, and play in San Francisco.

Technologists who join this effort will lead the City & County of San Francisco into the digital age, modernizing how we interact with residents citywide. The Roles

Product Manager

We are looking for an expert product manager to lead multi-disciplinary development of new user-focused digital services. While the products you work on may vary, our team is currently working on services that help people find affordable housing, start a business, find work, register as a cannabis business, find a pro-bono lawyer for immigration help, and more. As a Digital Services PM you’ll help manage stakeholders and the product team around common goals, manage delivery using disciplined agile methodology, and ensure that analytics and feedback are used to improve the product. You’ll also be a champion for user needs, improving business processes, and cutting bureaucratic red tape. Successful candidates will have leadership experience on agile product teams.

UX Content Writer (contract)

We are seeking an experienced UX content writer for short-term contract work. You'll be responsible for writing clear, simple, approachable language for transactional public services, including participating in user research to iteratively improve the content.

User Researcher (part-time)

We are seeking an experienced user researcher for short-term contract work. You'll be responsible for organizing, conducting, and synthesizing user research with the public and city staff to inform the development of digital services.

Housing Service Roles

Product Manager

We are looking for an expert product manager to join the housing digital services team. As the product manager you will coordinate a multi-disciplinary development effort to build new user-focused digital services for affordable housing. You’ll help manage stakeholders and the product team around common goals, manage delivery using disciplined agile methodology, and ensure that analytics and feedback are used to improve the product. You’ll also be a champion for user needs, improving business processes, and cutting bureaucratic red tape. Successful candidates will have leadership experience on agile product teams.

Full-Stack Engineer

We are seeking an outstanding, experienced senior full-stack engineer to help iteratively improve the affordable housing application service at housing.sfgov.org, as well as build new services to meet the needs of affordable housing seekers. Candidates must have strong skills in frontend and backend web development.

Salesforce Developer

We are seeking an outstanding, experienced senior Salesforce developer and technical architect to help operate, improve, and expand the Salesforce implementation that is used to manage the workflow for the Mayor’s Office of Housing and Community Development. Candidates must have strong skills in Salesforce development, including building an API for external users.

Designer

We are looking for an expert designer with broad experience with UX, UI, and visual design to join the new housing digital services team. As the housing team’s designer you will have an important role in continuing to uphold the high design bar we have set on housing.sfgov.org over the last two years. You will help lead user research, produce prototypes and designs, work closely with front-end developers.
